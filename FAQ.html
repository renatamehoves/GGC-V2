FAQ

The Photographer’s experience

1.How many weddings do you shoot per year on average?

Very often we’re asked “how many weddings do you do a year?” and we think it’s a great question to ask! Clients want to know for a few reasons, but mostly we think hearing this number assures them that 1.we’re real photographers who do this for an actual living and 2. we are smart about time management and don’t bite off more than we can chew. Other photographers ask us this question quite a bit as well, just out of curiosity. We’re always happy to answer! We learned early on in our career that finding the right balance is key, and so we decided to stick to a nice round number of weddings booked within a calendar year. A maximum number of weddings that wouldn’t overwhelm us, giving us the ability to give each couple that year the most amazing experience we can provide. This number also serves as a goal, or a way for us to keep track of how well we’re doing, and gauge our financial success (ability to pay for food and mortgages).

2. What do you enjoy about photographing weddings specifically?

Weddings are truly one of the most special moments in a person’s life. There is so much emotion all around and people seem to really put away their differences (for the most part) and come together to celebrate as a family. Being surrounded by all that love and warmth is something I really enjoy. The way a mom’s eyes mist up when she sees her son all grown up ready to start his own family, that instant when a dad realises that his little girl is not so little anymore, the first kiss between a married couple! What can I say – I am a hopeless romantic at heart and really enjoy being part of someone’s special day. There is always something going on and each wedding I photograph is different from the previous one. You get to spend the day capturing romance, laughter, joy, anticipation, excitement, and many other photographic moments for your clients.

The photographer’s style and approach

6. What makes your photography unique?

Weddings are a treasure chest of multiple stories built in throughout the day. As you photograph a wedding, you learn to recognise when a beautiful story is being played out right in front of you complete with characters, outfits, props, emotion and action. You just need to know where to look.

7. What level of editing can we expect, and what affects the look?

We are a true to the moment type of company, so you can expect true to the eye type of editing,some black and white and a little bit of matte.

8. Can I see a range of full wedding galleries?

Yes you can, we normally provide full galleries upon request, so that way our clients do not havew their private moments exposed.

9. What’s your favorite and least favorite part of the wedding day?

Our favorite part is when the groom sees the bride for the first time walking down the aisle or on a first look. this moment is always special. Our least favorite moment is when the party is over.

10. What are the values that you strive for in your brand?

We strive not only making sure you have every moment of the day captured but also on you two and your guests having a great experience with our photographers.
